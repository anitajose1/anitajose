About Me

I have always been drawn to design and user experience. Even in my previous role as an instructional designer, what I enjoyed the most was creating engaging user interactions that brought ideas to life visually while keeping navigation intuitive. After all, our brains love fancy things that are easy to use, right?


I decided to become a front-end developer because I like being at the intersection of design and logic. Before gaining programming skills, I was often limited to the functionalities of a software to build the interactions I wanted to create. But as a growing developer, I can now create the envisioned functionality and make it usable across platforms. I have been loving the web dev journey so far!
